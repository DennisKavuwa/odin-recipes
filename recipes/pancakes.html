Whole Wheat Pancake

Home

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 ½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour and sugar
  2. Add baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl
  3. store in an airtight container
  4. Mix 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 cup of milk
  5. Mix 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil together in a large bowl
  6. Cook pancakes on a hot griddle and serve with your choice of toppings
